STORY

When the clock strikes midnight, the Dark Hour begins. A hidden time that only a select few can experience, where humans turn into eerie coffins and otherworldly monsters called Shadows roam.

As a transfer student at Gekkoukan High School, you'll form bonds with your classmates, uncover the mysteries of the Dark Hour, and confront your destiny.
